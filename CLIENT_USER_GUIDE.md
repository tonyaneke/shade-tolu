## üì∏ Live Photo Upload Feature

### What is the Live Photo Upload?

The Live Photo Upload feature allows guests to share their wedding day photos in real-time. Photos uploaded by guests will automatically appear in the gallery section of the website, creating a beautiful collection of memories from your special day.

### How to Use (For Guests)

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop (Easiest)

1. Navigate to the **Gallery** page on the wedding website
2. Scroll down to find the **"Share Your Wedding Day Photos"** section
3. **Drag and drop** your photos directly onto the upload area
4. Wait for the upload to complete (you'll see a progress indicator)
5. Your photos will appear in the gallery immediately!

#### Method 2: Click to Upload

1. Go to the **Gallery** page
2. Find the upload section
3. **Click** on the upload area or the "Upload Photos" button
4. **Select** photos from your device (you can select multiple photos at once)
5. Wait for upload confirmation
6. Your photos will appear in the gallery!

### Important Guidelines for Photo Uploads

‚úÖ **What Works Best:**

- Photos taken on your smartphone or camera
- JPG, PNG, or other common image formats
- Photos up to 10MB in size
- Multiple photos can be uploaded at once

‚ùå **What to Avoid:**

- Videos (currently only photos are supported)
- Files larger than 10MB
- Very large files (they may take longer to upload)

### Tips for Wedding Day Photo Sharing

1. **Share in Real-Time**: Upload photos as you take them during the ceremony and reception
2. **Capture Special Moments**:

   - First dance
   - Cake cutting
   - Speeches
   - Candid moments
   - Family photos
   - Reception dancing

3. **Good Lighting**: Photos taken in good lighting will look better in the gallery

4. **Multiple Guests**: Encourage all guests to share their photos - the more perspectives, the better!

### How Photos Appear

- Photos are automatically optimized for web viewing
- They appear in a beautiful masonry/grid layout
- All photos are organized chronologically
- Photos are visible to all guests viewing the website

### Privacy Note

Photos uploaded to the gallery are visible to all guests who visit the website. Only upload photos you're comfortable sharing publicly with the wedding community.

## üíå RSVP System

### For Guests

1. **Navigate to RSVP Page**: Click on "RSVP" in the main navigation menu

2. **Enter Your Details**:

   - Your full name
   - Email address
   - Attendance status (Accepting / Declining)
   - Optional: Goodwill message for the couple

3. **Access Code**:

   - You'll receive an access code after submitting your RSVP
   - **Save this code** - it's your entry ticket to the wedding
   - The code is unique to you and validates entry for 1 person

4. **Important Reminders**:
   - Attendance is strictly by invitation
   - Access card validates entry for just 1 person
   - RSVP early to help the couple plan

### For Couple (Admin Access)

- View all RSVPs in the Admin Dashboard
- See statistics (total guests, acceptances, declines)
- Track RSVPs over time with charts
- Export guest list data
- View goodwill messages from guests

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Photo Upload Issues

**Problem**: Photos won't upload

- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- **Solution**: Make sure photos are under 10MB
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page and uploading again

**Problem**: Upload is slow

- **Solution**: This is normal for larger files - be patient
- **Solution**: Try uploading fewer photos at once

**Problem**: Photo doesn't appear immediately

- **Solution**: Refresh the page after a few seconds
- **Solution**: Check that upload completed successfully (you'll see a success message)

### RSVP Issues

**Problem**: Can't submit RSVP

- **Solution**: Make sure all required fields are filled
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- **Solution**: Try using a different browser

**Problem**: Lost access code

- **Solution**: Contact the couple directly - they can look it up in the admin dashboard

### General Issues

**Problem**: Website looks broken on mobile

- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page
- **Solution**: Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

**Problem**: Can't see photos in gallery

- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page
- **Solution**: Clear your browser cache if needed
